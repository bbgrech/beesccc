Welcome To Our Site !

Bee’s Chicken Coop Co-Op is dedicated to raising healthy, happy, free-range chickens…all for you !

Are you interested in having your own chicken, but don’t want the work?  Now, you can own your own **chicken(s), without the work !

Let us raise a chicken (or chickens) for you on our land, and you reap the benefits of fresh, healthy, organic eggs! (& healthy, happy, non-factory farmed chickens!)

We will raise a healthy chicken for you, in a loving and respectful, free-range environment, and you can benefit from the rewards! 

We feed our birds with only 100% organic, non-gmo feed++, which, we believe, makes the eggs produced healthier for you.+

Bird owners can even visit their chickens (when arrangements are made in advance), feed it organic table-scraps, watch it grow, and *eventually produce eggs.

Collect your eggs each weekend*. (Delivery service also available for an extra fee)

To learn more about our history and why we do what we do, please click “About Us“.

*An adult chicken usually will produce one egg per day, and usually takes one or two “days off” per week, yielding on average 4-6 eggs per week.  See FAQ for more info.

**Organic heritage turkeys also available. Raise your own organic turkey on our land!

***Fresh, organic eggs are also available for sale without purchasing a chicken. (delivery or pickup)  See Our Services page.

+This statement has not been evaluated or supported by the FDA

++ A list of the feed ingredients is available upon request.
